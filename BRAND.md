## Logo concept

- **Shape:** square (512×512), rounded corners 24%
- **Background:** deep midnight gradient — `#0b1220` → `#0a0f1c`, with a soft cyan/indigo glow
- **Foreground:** stylized download arrow forming the spine of a play triangle, flowing into a media wave
- **Typography:** modern geometric sans (Inter / SF Pro), letter `M` overlaid faintly behind the icon
- **Accents:** sky `#38bdf8` to indigo `#6366f1` linear gradient on the icon strokes
- **Vibe:** premium, calm, dark-mode native, Telegram-grade simplicity
